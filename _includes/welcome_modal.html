This is a test page. It maps the events listed in the ECPD blotters published on nixle from January 2019 to the latest blotter released. There is a gap from mid Feb 2019 to January 2020 where not blotters could be located.

The following is a summary of criminal activity in El Cerrito. It does not include all crimes. It also does not include non-criminal events such as missing persons, traffic collisions, non-criminal deaths, non-criminal disputes, nor many other police activities that do not result in arrest.

--------------

Clicking on the incident types to the left will show or hide that incident type on the map

There is also the option to display each incident or a heatmap of incidents

--------------

You can play through the records by day, week, or month by using the controls under the map.

you change the date range of incidents that are displayed with the slider on the bottom.
